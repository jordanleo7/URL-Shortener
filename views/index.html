URL Shortener Microservice

To shorten a URL, add "/new/http://www.yourwebsite.com" to the URL bar.
Example: "https://shorter-url.glitch.me/new/http://www.google.com"
Note: URLs must begin with http://, https://, or at least www.

To be redirected to a shortened URL, add the shortened URL to the URL bar.
Example: https://shorter-url.glitch.me/SaKxt will redirect your browser to "http://google.com".
